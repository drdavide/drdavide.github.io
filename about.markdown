My name is *Davide D'Amico*. Born and raised in Italy, with the passion of travels, music and digital technologies.
I like facing challenges with an interdisciplinary approach, lateral thinking and in a sustainable way.
I hold an M.Sc. in Energy Engineering. I have worked and studied in Italy, Austria, Thailand and in the U.K.
I am currently a PhD candidate in Digital Engineering at the [Cranfield University](https://www.cranfield.ac.uk/people/davide-damico-23152921), check out my [Likeding Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davide~damico/). Within my PhD project I am developing a *Digital Twin*, I have also created a Linked group (check out [Digital Twin Engineering Group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/13708020/)).
